Premodern Magic Gauntlet

Elves

March 19, 2026

A green creature combo deck that generates absurd amounts of mana through Priest of Titania and Gaea’s Cradle, then uses Survival of the Fittest to assemble whatever creature combination it needs. Tangle Wire locks opponents out while the elves take over the board.

Enchantress

March 19, 2026

A white-green enchantment combo deck. Argothian Enchantress and Enchantress’s Presence draw a card for every enchantment cast, creating an engine that runs itself. Serra’s Sanctum taps for enormous mana. Sterling Grove and Solitary Confinement protect the setup. Opalescence turns all enchantments into creatures and swings for the win.

Goblins

March 19, 2026

The classic Premodern goblin tribal deck. Goblin Lackey and Goblin Warchief enable blisteringly fast starts. Goblin Matron tutors the right tool, Goblin Ringleader refills the hand, and Goblin Piledriver closes games in one swing. Wasteland and Rishadan Port keep opponents off their mana.

OathPonza

March 19, 2026

A green-red land destruction deck. Mox Diamond accelerates into early Thermokarst and Winter’s Grasp while Wasteland and Rishadan Port chip in. Terravore grows enormous from all the lands in graveyards. Oath of Druids provides an alternate engine, and Sphere of Resistance slows spell-heavy decks to a crawl.

Psychatog

March 19, 2026

A blue-black control deck built entirely around Psychatog as its win condition. A web of cantrips (Portent, Opt, Gush, Accumulated Knowledge) filters through the deck and fills the graveyard. Duress and Counterspell answer threats. Psychatog wins by exiling hand and graveyard cards to become a one-shot kill.

Stiflenought

March 19, 2026

A mono-blue combo-control deck. Phyrexian Dreadnought enters the battlefield with a trigger to sacrifice creatures totaling 12 power — Stifle or Vision Charm counters that trigger, leaving a 12/12 trampler in play for a single mana. The rest of the deck protects it with Counterspell, Foil, and cantrips.
