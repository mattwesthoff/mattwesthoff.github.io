A six-deck gauntlet for playing [Premodern](https://www.premodernmagic.com/) — a format using cards printed between 1995 and 2003. These decks cover a range of archetypes: aggro, combo, control, and prison. Pick a deck, sit down across from someone, and find out whose plan breaks first.
