A mono-blue combo-control deck with one goal: get a 12/12 trampling Phyrexian Dreadnought into play for one mana.

When Dreadnought enters, it triggers a "sacrifice creatures with total power 12 or greater" ability. Stifle counters that trigger entirely. Vision Charm phases the Dreadnought out before the trigger resolves, then it phases back in at the start of the next turn with no memory of what happened. Either way: 12/12 trampler, one mana spent.

The rest of the deck — Counterspell, Foil, Daze, Thwart — makes sure the Dreadnought survives long enough to connect. Gush and Accumulated Knowledge keep the hand full. Portent and Opt set up the next draw.
